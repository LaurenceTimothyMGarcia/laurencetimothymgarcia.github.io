Design Analysis of DPS Idle

Introduction
    DPS Idle is a free to play idle game where your character explores a dungeon, defeating enemies, and unlocks cards to defeat stronger enemies. While 
being a very simple game on the surface, there are multiple components to this game that makes it an addicting time for players to enjoy.

Core Gameplay Loop
Notes
Defeat monster, gain income, Use income to buy more cards or upgrades, repeat
Outer Loop
Get Income or deal damage
Join Guild
Multiplier for income/damage depending on guild
Leave Guild to start fresh with new multiplier on damage and income
Repeat

Space, Objects w/ Attributes, Actions
Notes
Space
The monster on the screen with health and worth
The cards or spheres of magic
Upgrade screens, Guilds, Heroes, etc.
Objects (Attributes)
Player: Gold, Leafs, Cards, Silver Blue Coins, Skulls, Total DPS
Cards: Type, Color, Function
Enemies: Gold Worth, Hit Points
Chest: Color, Cost
Guilds: Multiplier and upgrades
Heroes: Upgrades
Achievements: Unlocked
Quests: Completed
Actions
Click Monster: Deals damage to monster
Open Chest: Uses player gold to give a random card, price increases after every few purchases.
Spheres of Magic: click on spheres to add a damage multiplier to the player
Enhance Random Card: Use a leaf or a silver blue coin to upgrade a card
Shop: Use real money to buy gems for more powerful upgrades
Join Guild: Join a guild to have a multiplier on damage or income
Leave Guild: Restart game but with the multiplier gained from time invested from Guild
Choose Hero: Choose a hero after meeting a specific criteria then select upgrades

Goal of the game, and how player decisions affect reaching the goal
Notes
To defeat each enemy and move on to the next one to clear the dungeon
Player can choose
What to spend their leafs and silver blue coins on
What deck of cards to spend their gold on
What cards to upgrade
When to leave the guild for a new run
Which hero to unlock

Effect of luck and skill on the game
Notes
Cards are selected randomly
At some point the player hits a roadblock and thats when they should leave the guild to restart the game.
