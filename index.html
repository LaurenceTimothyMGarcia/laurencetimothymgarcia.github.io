Hobbies and Interests

3D Modeling
I have been 3D modeling ever since the end of 7th grade. Starting from Blender, I would slowly move towards AutoCad and Solidworks in high school as I wanted to try engineering design at the time. However, in my junior year of high school I would transition back to 3D modeling in Blender for 3D printing models, game development assets, or just personal projects to help improve my own skills. All of my projects I post on my Instagram @emergencyartist or my Artstation portfolio.
Video Games
I love to play games of a wide range of genres. From JRPGs, like Pokemon and Persona, to Rougelikes, like Hades and Risk of Rain, to competitive multiplayer games, such as Smash Bros, League of Legends, Apex Legends, and Overwatch, the multitude of games I have played enables me to experience various worlds and visions created by designers. It also has given me appreciation of various game mechanics and design techniques that game designers use in order to provide an experience to the player.
Video Production
The video creation process is something I have always wanted to do. I create my own personal YouTube videos of any fun idea I think of, whether its a fake product I am "advertising", reviewing video games, or just a fun vlog. I have also used my video creation skills to help with club related stuff, such as creating the advertisement for our Halloween 2021 Game Jam.
